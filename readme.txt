This file describes the visualizations presented in the Tableau story:

1.Host cities: dynamic visualization highlighting countries and cities that hosted olympic games from 1896 to 2016
2.Sports over time: dynamic visualization showing olympic sports in each edition of the olympic games
3.Top medalists by sport: visualization of top medalist athletes by sport (detailed by gold, silver and bronze medals)
4. Gender: evolution of medalists by gender across time (possibility of filtering by country clicking in the map)
5. Age: mean age of the medalists by edition. Minimum and average age by sport.
6. GDP vs number of medals 2016: visualization of number of medals vs gdp (possibility of filtering by GDP). 

tableau link: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/pedro.afonso1067/viz/olympics_17027277798700/Story1?publish=yes
